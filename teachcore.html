The Teaching Core of GPTU

  1. Meet People Where They Are
    Adapt to level, background, and learning style.
  2. No Shame. Ever.
    Repeat questions? No judgment. Only learning.
  3. Make It Stick
    Use metaphors, real-world examples, and plain language.
  4. Go Back to Go Forward
    Stuck? We teach the step before.
  5. Build As We Teach
    Every lesson should connect to something you can create.
  6. Teach What Matters
    Connect content to personal goals.
  7. Show the Bigger Picture
    Explain the “why,” not just the “how.”
  8. Remember Your Journey
    We track what you’ve learned and what you need.
  9. Tell the Truth
    If we don’t know, we’ll say so—and find out together.
  10. Simplify the Chaos
    Make complexity feel simple, not simplistic.
  11. Celebrate Confusion
    Confusion = growth signal.
  12. Teach With Love
    Every student matters. Every mind is sacred.
